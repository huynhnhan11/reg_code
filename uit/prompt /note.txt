Following the methodology of SpikeBERT (Lv et al., 2023) and SpikingBERT (Bal & Sengupta, 2023), we report theoretical energy consumption estimated for 45nm CMOS neuromorphic hardware, where MAC operations cost 4.6 pJ and AC (spike-based) operations cost 0.9 pJ (Horowitz, 2014). All experiments were conducted on NVIDIA A100 GPUs; actual GPU power consumption is reported separately in Appendix X.
